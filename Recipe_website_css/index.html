Aloo Jeera

Minimum Ingredients

  • Boiled potatoes
  • cumin seeds
  • turmeric

Recipe

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds till they splutter.
  2. Add turmeric and diced boiled potatoes.
  3. Add salt and sauté for 4–5 minutes until golden and slightly crispy.

Vegetable Upma

Minimum Ingredients

  • Rava
  • mustard seeds
  • mixed veggies

Recipe

  1. Roast rava lightly and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil, splutter mustard seeds, and sauté green chilies and chopped veggies.
  3. Add water, bring to boil, add rava slowly while stirring, and cook till thick.
